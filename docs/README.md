# QR Anything

## About

QR Anything is a friendly, full-featured Android app that makes working with QR codes simple. Create professional QR codes in seconds, scan any code instantly, and keep everything organized in one place.

## What you can do

- Generate QR codes from everyday things: website links, text, Wi‑Fi, contacts, emails, phone numbers, events, and more
- Scan QR codes quickly and accurately with a smooth camera experience
- See rich details for every code with smart actions
- Save QR codes to your gallery as high‑quality images and share them anywhere
- Keep a complete history of everything you scan or create
- Customize the app to your liking with themes and settings
- Send feedback or suggestions right from the app

## Why it’s useful

- Save time: create and scan in seconds with clear, guided flows
- Be organized: everything you scan or generate is remembered, searchable, and easy to manage
- Look professional: export clean, high‑quality QR images ready for print or sharing
- Stay private: your data lives on your device; you control what you share

## Feature highlights

### QR Code Generation
- Create QR codes instantly from text, URLs, Wi‑Fi credentials, contact cards (vCard), emails, phone numbers, SMS, calendar events, and more
- Real‑time preview with smooth animations
- Clean, high‑quality QR rendering suitable for print
- One‑tap sharing to your favorite apps

### Camera Scanner
- Fast, accurate scanning powered by modern barcode recognition
- Real‑time detection with a helpful scanning overlay
- Flashlight toggle for low‑light environments
- Scan from photos: import images from your gallery
- Auto‑detect URLs and open them with one tap

### QR Details Screen
- Rich details for each QR/Barcode: clearly formatted content and type
- Smart actions based on content: open links, connect to Wi‑Fi, call or message contacts, copy text, share, and more
- Supports many code types: QR Code, Aztec, Data Matrix, PDF417, EAN/UPC, Code 39/93/128, and other common symbologies

### History & Organization
- Automatic history for all scanned and generated codes
- Powerful search to find what you need quickly
- Filters to switch between generated vs. scanned items
- Quick actions: copy, re‑share, edit (where applicable), or delete
- Beautiful, timestamped cards for easy browsing

### Export & Sharing
- Save any QR as a high‑quality PNG image to your gallery
- Share images or content directly to other apps
- Copy content to your clipboard in a tap

### Personalization & Settings
- Theme selection: Light, Dark, or follow System
- App information and version details
- Easy access to Terms of Use and Privacy Policy
- Manage optional analytics preferences

### Feedback
- Built‑in feedback form for suggestions and bug reports
- Submit feedback directly from the app

### Accessibility & Internationalization
- Designed with accessibility in mind for a smooth experience
- Multi‑language interface with right‑to‑left support where applicable

## Get started

1. Install the app on your Android device
2. Open the app and choose “Generate” or “Scan” from the home screen
3. Save, share, and manage your codes from History whenever you need them
