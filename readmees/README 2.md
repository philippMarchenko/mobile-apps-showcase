### Why do I need location permission?

**Answer:**
Android and iOS require location permission for WiFi scanning due to privacy regulations. WiFi Scanner Pro doesn't actually track your location - this permission is only used to scan for WiFi networks. It's a system requirement, not our choice!

---

### Why can't I see device hostnames?

**Answer:**
Some devices don't broadcast hostnames, or they may be on a different subnet. You'll still see their IP address, MAC address, and manufacturer (when available).

---

### How accurate is the speed test?

**Answer:**
Our speed test is very accurate and uses industry-standard test methods. Results may vary based on:
- Network congestion
- Distance from router
- Number of connected devices
- ISP throttling or traffic shaping
- Test server selection

Run multiple tests at different times for the most accurate picture.

### Why do I need location permission?

**Answer:**
Android and iOS require location permission for WiFi scanning due to privacy regulations. WiFi Scanner Pro doesn't actually track your location - this permission is only used to scan for WiFi networks. It's a system requirement, not our choice!

---

### Why can't I see device hostnames?

**Answer:**
Some devices don't broadcast hostnames, or they may be on a different subnet. You'll still see their IP address, MAC address, and manufacturer (when available).

---

### How accurate is the speed test?

**Answer:**
Our speed test is very accurate and uses industry-standard test methods. Results may vary based on:
- Network congestion
- Distance from router
- Number of connected devices
- ISP throttling or traffic shaping
- Test server selection

Run multiple tests at different times for the most accurate picture.
